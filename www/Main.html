About Us

Mission:
"To establish a collaborative commons with emphasis on map-based educational technology for the benefit of botanic gardens, museums, zoos, parks, private lands, campuses, and governmental organizations.”

Maps convey information quickly and effectively. Maps help us organize data and visualize relationships. Maps are incredibly powerful in directing us and helping us answer questions prefaced with where, when, and how. Geogardens is dedicated to having maps and their data become a central and fundamental component of the nonprofit organization.

Join with us and together we’ll show the way.
